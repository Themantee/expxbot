Ethereum Arbitrage Bot

An arbitrage bot on Ethereum is a trading bot that exploits price differences for the same asset across multiple decentralized exchanges (DEXs). It automatically buys the asset on the cheaper exchange and sells it on the more expensive one to make a profit..

Ethereum Sniper Bot

A sniper bot is an automated bot that instantly buys newly launched tokens at the exact moment they appear on a decentralized exchange. The goal is to get in before retail traders, securing the lowest possible price..

Ethereum MEV Bot

An MEV bot exploits the structure of Ethereum transactions to profit from other traders' transactions by front-running, back-running, or sandwiching their trades..
